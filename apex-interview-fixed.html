Welcome to APEX INTERVIEW. We built this platform to help you prepare for real interviews through realistic AI-powered practice — and that means we handle sensitive information like your resume and mock interview performance. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and the control you have over it.

1

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use APEX INTERVIEW, we may collect the following categories of information:

Identity details — your full name, email address, profession, and country.
Resume files you upload (PDF or DOCX) for analysis and personalised question generation.
Interview responses and scores recorded during your mock interview sessions.
Account information, including login credentials and interview preferences.
Feedback you voluntarily submit to help us improve the platform.
2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect strictly to operate and improve APEX INTERVIEW:

Create and manage your account
Provide interview preparation and AI resume analysis services
Generate personalised interview questions drawn from your own resume
Display your interview reports, performance scores, and dashboard analytics
Improve our platform, AI models, and overall user experience
Contact you regarding product updates, account notices, and customer support

Resume & Interview Data

Any resume or interview data you upload is used solely to provide the service you requested — generating questions, evaluating your answers, and producing your readiness reports.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, resume content, or interview recordings with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes — ever.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures — including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and routine security reviews — to protect your data from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to contact us immediately at support.apexinterview@gmail.com if you suspect any unauthorised access to your account.

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how the platform is used. When you first visit APEX INTERVIEW, a cookie consent banner will appear — allowing you to accept all cookies or customise which non-essential categories are active.

7

Third-Party Services

APEX INTERVIEW relies on carefully selected third-party providers for hosting, database management, analytics, authentication, payment processing, and communication. These providers only receive the minimum data necessary to perform their specific function and are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection obligations.

We do not use third-party services that engage in cross-site tracking or behavioural advertising targeting our users.
8

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have rights over your personal data under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and, for users in the European Union, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These rights include:

Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
Right to correction — request that inaccurate or outdated information be corrected
Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
Right to withdraw consent — opt out of non-essential data processing at any time
Right to portability — receive a copy of your data in a portable format, where applicable
To exercise any of these rights, email us at support.apexinterview@gmail.com. We aim to respond to all verified requests within 30 days.
9

Children's Privacy

APEX INTERVIEW is intended for users aged 13 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received such data, we will take prompt steps to delete it and notify the relevant guardian where possible.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you directly via email before the changes take effect.
